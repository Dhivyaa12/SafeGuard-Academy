# **App Name**: SafeGuard Academy

## Core Features:

- AI Training Recommendations: AI-powered tool to recommend personalized training modules based on staff roles (chef, front desk, housekeeping, etc.)
- AR Fire Simulation: Interactive AR training videos simulating hotel fire scenarios (kitchen, corridor, guest room), with interactive elements like selecting the correct extinguisher and finding emergency exits.
- Gamified Learning: Gamified learning paths where staff earn badges as they progress through training modules, encouraging engagement and completion.
- Knowledge Assessment: Post-training assessments including MCQs and scenario-based questions to test knowledge and retention of fire safety procedures.
- Certification: Certification generation that automatically generates certificates after an employee successfully passes a training module assessment.
- AI Chat Coach: AI Chat Coach provides personalized recommendations to hotel staff. Example prompts: 'What extinguisher to use for electric fire?' or 'Focus more on grease fire protocols'.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, calming blue (#3B5998) to inspire trust and security.
- Background color: Very light desaturated blue (#E6EBF2), same hue as primary color to maintain cohesion, creating a comfortable, unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: A contrasting, muted green (#8FBC8F) drawn from analogous position on color wheel to represent safety, used sparingly to draw attention to key interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif to combine modernity with warmth for easy readability.
- Use clear, representative icons related to fire safety.
- Clean, card-based design with a focus on readability and accessibility.
